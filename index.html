Nike Air Max 97 Ultra

The New Authority in Footwear

A classic gets an 'Ultra' upgrade, these men's Air Max 97 Ultra trainers from Nike are a footwear essential. Inspired by the sleek look of high-speed Japanese bullet trains, these kicks have been kitted out with a durable textile upper, which ensures feet feel fresh and comfortable. With the famed Waffle pattern outsole for grippy traction, a mini Swoosh shows to the sidewall, while jacquard detailing adds a further style twist. Sat on a full-length Air unit for supreme cushioning, they're finished with OG Air Max 97 branding to the inner sole bed and tongue.
